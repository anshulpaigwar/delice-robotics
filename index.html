How it started
Our Story

Why we built Delice.

As students it was hard to balance studies and cooking, with few options for low-cost, healthy, 24×7 food. That struggle followed us to work — early flights, late nights, offices far from good food. The idea of a robotic chef sparked from there. We gathered techies, foodies and people passionate about change, and Delice Robotics was born.
